# Founding Member Welcome Email

**Type:** Transactional / Relationship  
**Trigger:** User joins the Pawgress waitlist and secures a Founding Member spot (first 50 signups)  
**Goal:** Make them feel genuinely valued, confirm their perks, and build excitement for launch

---

## Subject Line Options

1. `You're a Pawgress Founding Member 🐾`
2. `You made it. Here's what that means for you.`
3. `One of 50. Here's what you've unlocked.`

**Recommended:** Option 1 — clear, personal, milestone-marking without being hype-y.

**Preview text:** `Your spot is locked in. Here's everything that comes with it.`

---

## Email Body

---

**Subject:** You're a Pawgress Founding Member 🐾

---

Hi [First Name],

I mean it when I say this — thank you.

You didn't wait to see how it played out. You didn't need a polished product or a wall of reviews. You believed in what I'm building before most people even knew it existed. That means a lot to me.

You're one of the first 50 trainers on the Pawgress waitlist. That makes you a Founding Member — and I don't take that lightly.

---

**Here's what you've locked in:**

**3 months completely free** — when I launch, you're on me. No charge, no catch. Three full months to use Pawgress exactly how you would with a paid plan.

**Early access** — you'll get in before the public. No waitlist, no queue. You're first through the door.

**Exclusive feature updates** — as I build, you'll hear about it. You'll know what's coming before anyone else does.

**Launch pricing, locked in forever** — whatever the launch price is, that's your price. Always. Even as Pawgress grows and pricing evolves, yours doesn't.

**Founding Member profile badge** — inside Pawgress, you'll carry a badge that marks you as one of the originals. A small thing, maybe. But it's real, and it's yours.

---

I built Pawgress because I wanted to build something that actually matters. I did my research and couldn't find a single tool built around how dog trainers actually work. As a dog owner and someone who genuinely loves dogs, I knew I wanted to be the one to fill that gap.

I'm not done building yet, but I'm moving fast — and now you're part of it.

I'll be in touch as I get closer to launch. Until then, know that your spot is secure, your perks are locked in, and I'm genuinely grateful you're with me on this.

Talk soon,

**Dana**  
Founder, Pawgress  
[pawgress.io](https://pawgress.io) · [@getpawgress](https://instagram.com/getpawgress)

_Train. Track. Grow. 🐾_

## Notes

- Swap `[First Name]` with merge tag from your email platform (e.g. `{{first_name}}` in Mailchimp / `{first_name}` in ConvertKit)
- Send from a personal name if possible (e.g. "Dana at Pawgress") — increases open rates and feels more founder-to-trainer
- Plain text format preferred over heavy HTML design — keeps the personal tone intact
- Do not add more CTAs — this email is a relationship moment, not a conversion push

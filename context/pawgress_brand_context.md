# Pawgress Brand Context

## What is Pawgress?

Pawgress is a client management SaaS platform built specifically for professional dog trainers.It helps trainers manage their entire business in one place — clients, dogs, training sessions, notes, progress tracking, and roadmaps.

## The Problem We Solve

Most professional dog trainers are running their business through a chaotic mix of WhatsApp, spreadsheets, sticky notes, and memory. They're spending valuable time on admin instead of doing what they love — training dogs Pawgress gives them a professional, organized home for their entire business.

## Who We Serve

Professional dog trainers who:

- Run their own independent training business
- Are serious about their work and clients
- Want to look and operate more professionally
- Are tired of scattered notes and admin chaos
- Care deeply about their dogs and clients

## Mission & Tagline

- To help dog trainers spend less time on admin and more time doing what they love.
- Train. Track. Grow.

## Our Pillars

1. We understand the grind — we know what it's like to run a dog training business day to day
2. Professional tools deserve professional trainers — we believe trainers deserve software built for them
3. Progress is everything — every session, every dog, every client deserves to be tracked properly
4. We love this world — genuine passion for dogs and the people who train them

## Market

- 50,000-100,000+ professional dog trainers in the US
- Mostly self-employed, running their own business
- Growing industry with 16% projected growth
- Largely underserved by existing software tools

## Competitors

BarkBook (barkbook.app) — focuses on scheduling and booking automation. Does not have training specific features like session summaries, homework, progress tracking or roadmaps. Pawgress owns the training management space.

## Our Edge

Unlike generic CRMs or booking tools, Pawgress is built specifically for the training workflow:

- Session summaries with scores
- Client homework tracking
- Training roadmaps per dog
- Goal setting and progress tracking
- Everything a trainer actually needs

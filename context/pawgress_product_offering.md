# Pawgress Product Offering

## Core Features

### Dashboard

The trainer's home base.

- Overview of upcoming sessions this week
- Overview of recently completed sessions
- Overview of all active clients
- Quick access to create new client or session
- Personalized greeting ("Good morning, [name]")

### Owner Management

Complete profiles for every client.

- Owner personal details (name, email, phone, location)
- Notes on the owner
- Goals for their dog's training
- Training roadmap overview
- Total payments received from this owner
- All dogs linked to this owner
- Complete session history

### Dog Profiles

Each dog gets their own detailed profile.

- Dog name, breed, age, photo
- Notes specific to the dog
- Individual training roadmap
- Session history
- Progress over time. One owner can have multiple dogs. Each dog has their own independent profile and training journey.

### Session Management

#### Upcoming Sessions

Schedule sessions in advance:

- Select which dog
- Date, time, and location
- Session title
- Price
- Google Calendar sync

#### Session Summaries

After each session the trainer fills in:

- What was achieved
- Session score (1-10)
- What to work on next
- Homework for the owner
- Session cost

#### Session Display

Every completed session shows:

- Dog photo and name
- Date, time, location
- Session title and cost
- Full summary
- Score
- Homework assigned

### Goals

Set and track training goals per owner.

- Display top 3 goals prominently
- Track progress toward each goal

### Training Roadmap

A customizable training plan per dog.

- Built and edited by the trainer
- Visible on the dog's profile
- Shows the full training journey from start to finish

### Payment Overview

Simple payment tracking per owner.

- Log session costs
- See total revenue per client
- No payment processing — purely for record keeping and business overview

### Settings

- Trainer profile (name, photo, email)
- Password management
- Notification preferences
- Contact form for bugs and feedback
- Manage subscription

# Pawgress Instagram Kickstart Content Plan

**Period:** April 8–21, 2026
**Goal:** Build brand awareness, establish Pawgress as a peer-leader in the dog training community, and drive waitlist signups.
**Phase:** Pre-launch, early audience building.
**Cadence:** 5 Feed posts/week + daily Stories + 2 Reels/week

---

## Content Pillar Mix

| Pillar                             | % of Feed | Rationale                                        |
| ---------------------------------- | --------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Pain Points                        | 30%       | Hook the right people by mirroring their reality |
| Feature Highlights (benefit-first) | 25%       | Build product desire without being salesy        |
| Dog Love / Community               | 20%       | Earn goodwill, algorithm favor, saves            |
| Behind the Scenes                  | 15%       | Build trust and emotional investment pre-launch  |
| CTA / Waitlist                     | 10%       | Convert warm audience — never lead with this     |

## Feed Carousel — 6 Slides

### Slide 1 — Hook

For a long time, I knew I wanted to build something. Not a side project — something that actually mattered. I just couldn't figure out what that was. Until I looked at my dog.

---

### Slide 2 — The Search

I went through every topic I cared about — fitness, travel, dogs. I kept coming back to dogs every single time. Not because it made the most sense on paper. Because it was the only one that felt right.

---

### Slide 3 — The Anchor

My dog is my everything. And once I started looking at the dog training world, I couldn't stop seeing the same thing — trainers doing serious, skilled work with almost nothing built to support them.

---

### Slide 4 — It Clicked

I'm a developer. I've built client management tools before. And I genuinely love dogs. When I looked at what dog trainers were missing, I realised I already knew how to build it. So I did.

---

### Slide 5 — The Product

Pawgress is a client management platform built specifically for dog trainers. Session summaries, per-dog profiles, training roadmaps, homework tracking — everything a trainer actually needs, in one place. No more scattered notes. No more WhatsApp threads. Just a clean, professional home for your entire business.

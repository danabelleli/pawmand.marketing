# Founder Story — True Version

**Date: April 9, 2026**

---

## The Real Story (in Dana's words)

Dana is a software developer who always dreamed of building something of her own. She loves problem solving and creating something from nothing. For a long time she wanted to build a product that brings real value to people's lives — but didn't know what. Every idea that came up wasn't the right one.

She brainstormed topics she genuinely cared about: fitness, dogs, travel. Dogs kept pulling her back — because she has a dog, and her dog is her everything.

She started looking at what she could build in the dog world. Landed on a webapp for dog trainers — because she's organised, she has experience building client management applications, and it was the right combination: something she loves, something she's good at.

She built Pawmand hoping to make a difference — not just for trainers, but for dog owners too. As a dog owner herself, she understands both sides.
